Week 10: Workshop / Feedback Day

← Back to Course Home

Goals

  • Get direct feedback on your project from the instructor and peers
  • Identify gaps in your experiments and analysis
  • Refine your research narrative and paper framing
  • Plan remaining experiments and triangulation strategies

Workshop Format

This session is dedicated to working on your projects with hands-on support. Each team will have time to:

  • Present current results (5 min per team): Show your latest plots and findings
  • Receive feedback (10 min per team): Instructor and peer feedback on methodology, framing, and next steps
  • Work time: Use remaining time to address feedback and run experiments

Preparation

Come prepared with:

  • Your most important experimental results so far
  • A list of open questions or blockers
  • A draft outline of your paper's story arc

Project Milestone

Due: Thursday of Week 10

  • Continue triangulation: scale or diversity experiments
  • Finalize core experiment results
  • Begin drafting introduction and methods sections
